Let your visitors find you

Make sure you care about Search Engine Optimization already while creating the first contents. This means you should fill in the Meta Tags to every article you publish. Further we recommend to announce your website to all bigger search engines (Google, Yahoo, MSN etc.). If you do so, the search engine robots will be faster in visiting and indexing your website. With Contrexx you’re able to track all robots activity with the Contrexx Statistics.
