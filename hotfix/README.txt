English:
To apply the hotfix(es) contained in this archive, copy
the whole content of this hotfix folder into your
Contrexx installation. Then, move all files and directories
from the files subdirectory to your installation. As a
last step, point your browser to the URL of your installation
and add /hotfix to it (example: http://yoursite.com/hotfix).
As you see, the hotfix system will show you which hotfixes 
were applied.
